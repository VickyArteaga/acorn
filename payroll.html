Payroll

Save time, and avoid missed deadlines.

Managing payroll in house is not a straightforward task. Our team of experts is available to manage the day to day tasks, removing any anxiety of missing deadlines and associated fines.

Payroll made easy.

Running a payroll in house is becoming an increasingly complicated task. Not only it is time consuming, but employers are expected to cope with statutory sick pay, statutory maternity pay and student loan repayments. From April 2013 real time submissions are now required increasing the need for a well managed and efficient service.

A payroll service adapted to your needs.

Acorn Payroll Services provide a payroll processing service to meet your needs, whether that be a weekly or monthly payroll. Utilising HMRC accredited software, we can act as your agent with the HMRC, saving you time and money.

Our payroll services start from as little as £35 per month. The service includes:

Payslips for employees
Weekly / Monthly payroll reports
Calculation of statutory sick, maternity and paternity pay
All in year online filing including P45 for employees leaving
Implementing PAYE tax code changes
Online filing of year end summaries (P35 & P60)
